  • Where: LT-PCSA
  • When: 6 pm, 2nd August

How to raise money - Entrepreneurship Bootcamp

The speaker for today is Mr. Piyush Surana (http://linkedin.com/in/piyush-surana). This lead investor at the tomorrow capital, has helped growing business take on challenges by making sense of big data and putting structures around their inherent chaos. Wealth Investment Workshop

The ET Wealth Investment Workshop aims to establish a deeper understanding and awareness about the prevailing trends and safe practices in the markets amongst investors. The Investment Workshop will bring together key stakeholders from across the market and will focus on investor education which would guide the participants on the art of financial goal setting and investment options to secure their future and retire rich.
